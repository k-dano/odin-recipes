Dutch-Oven Bread

Description

Simple ingredients for simple bread recipe

Ingredients

  • 1½ cup warm water
  • 1 sachet or 2½ tsp of dry active yeast
  • pinch of sugar
  • 3 cups of flour (at least 2 cups all purpose, up to 1 cup whole wheat)
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Check yeast is alive by combining yeast and sugar into warm water.
  2. You should see a foam layer form to indicate yeast is alive, if not replace yeast.
  3. Combine flour and and salt into a large bowl and quickly mix.
  4. Add wet ingredients and mix well, dough should be slightly sticky to wet for this bread
  5. knead well for 5 minutes
  6. Cover with camp cloth and let rise for 1½ hours to 2 hours in warm place. If room is cold, the oven with the bulb on will work well.
  7. You should expect dough to be puffy, gently fold and rotate onto itself 4-6 times then let rise again for 20 minutes
  8. Line dutch oven with baking paper, fold dough again and tuck underneath to create a ball, then place into dutch oven
  9. Cover dutch oven with damp cloth and let rise for 20 minutes one final time, in the meantime set oven to 270°C and place lid inside
  10. Once 20 minutes has passed,cut lines into top of dough in desired shape, use oven-mitts to place lid ontop and set onto the largest burner on highest setting
  11. Once dutch oven is hot to touch, place inside oven and bake for 30 minutes
  12. Remove lid and continue another 10 minutes
  13. Remove pot from oven and place loaf on cooling rack
  14. Let cool for at least 45 minutes before cutting and enjoying!
