Chocolate Octopus Cookies

Background info

An old recipe I remember making as a child from a singapore airlines kids member magazine. Basically a sugar cookie with cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 125g butter, softened
  • 125g sugar (brown or white)
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbs cocoa powder
  • 175g flour

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and sugar
  2. Add egg and cocoa, mix until combined
  3. Add flour in small amounts while continuing to mix
  4. Chill dough roughly 30 minutes to make it easy to work with
  5. Preheat oven to 160°C
  6. Divide dough into 6-8 balls (the rough amount you can make). I recommend working with half of the dough at a time and keeping the other half in the fridge.
  7. Make a log shape and flatten to about 1cm thickness, make slices half way up cookie and shape into arms
  8. Bake for 20 minutes
  9. Cool and decorate as desired, or eat straight up cause you a busy fellow and don't got no time for dat
