Peanut Butter & Banana Doggie Bikkies

Description

Fun-looking recipe for peanut butter and banana biscuits for good dogs

Ingrediets

  • 1 egg
  • ⅓ cup peanut butter
  • ½ cup mashed banana
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • ½ cup wheat germ
  • 1 egg white, lightly beaten, for brushing

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Lightly grease baking sheet.
  2. Stir together the egg, peanut butter, banana, and honey in a medium bowl; blend thoroughly.
  3. Stir in the flour and wheat germ; mix well.
  4. Turn dough out onto a floured board and roll to 1/4 inch thick.
  5. Cut into desired shapes with a cookie cutter, place on prepared baking sheet, and brush tops with egg white.
  6. Bake biscuits until dried and golden brown, approx 30 minutes.
  7. Once done, remove and cool on wire rack.
